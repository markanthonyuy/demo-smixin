What is Smixin?

Smixin - (Smart-Mixing-Inside)
Our patented technology that mixes
and doses soap, water and air to create
a new handwashing experience.

The all-in-one contactless handwashing system
allow a proper and comfortable handwashing using
90% less water, up to 60% less soap, and paper.

Thanks to its design, Smixin handwashing system
can be installed wherever hand hygiene is needed.
Inside as well as outside of the washroom.
